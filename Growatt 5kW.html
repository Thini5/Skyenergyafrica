Growatt 5kW Off-Grid Inverter

Growatt 5kW Off-Grid Inverter | SPF 5000US | UL 1741

Growatt 5000-US

Growatt 5000-US multifunctional off-grid solar inverter, integrated with a MPPT solar charge controller, a high-frequency pure sine wave inverter, and a UPS function module all in one machine. Perfect for off-grid backup power and self-consumption applications. This Unit is 240V output only. For your 120V loads you will need the Growatt 5kW Split Phase Transformer | ATS 5000T-ES

Features:

  • Rated power 5KW, power factor 1
  • MPPT ranges 120V~430V, 450VDC
  • High-frequency inverter with small size and lightweight
  • Pure sine wave AC output
  • Solar and utility grid can power loads at the same time
  • With CAN/RS485 for BMS communication
  • Parallel operation up to 6 unit (only with battery connected)
  • WIFI/ GPRS remote monitoring (optional)
